Manali - Paradise in the Himalayas

Nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is one of India's most popular hill stations.

About Manali

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in India's northern Himachal Pradesh state. It has a reputation as a backpacking center and honeymoon destination. It's a gateway for skiing in the Solang Valley and trekking in Parvati Valley. It's also a jumping-off point for paragliding, rafting and mountaineering in the Pir Panjal mountains.

Best Time to Visit

October to June is the best time to visit Manali. The weather is pleasant and ideal for adventure activities. December to February is perfect for snow lovers.

Key Attractions

  • Solang Valley - Adventure sports
  • Hidimba Devi Temple - Ancient cave temple
  • Rohtang Pass - Spectacular mountain views
  • Old Manali - Charming village area
  • Jogini Waterfall - Beautiful natural waterfall

Travel Information

Distance from Delhi: 540 km
Journey Time by Bus: 12-14 hours
Best For: Honeymooners, adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers

Goa - Beaches, Sun and Fun

India's party capital with beautiful beaches, Portuguese heritage, and vibrant nightlife.

About Goa

Goa is India's smallest state and known for its endless beaches, stellar nightlife, eclectic seafood, world-heritage listed architecture. Spread along the west coast of India, Goa was a Portuguese colony until 1961, which explains the strong Mediterranean influence in the architecture and culture.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit Goa when the weather is pleasant and perfect for beach activities. The monsoon season from June to September offers lush green landscapes.

Key Attractions

  • Calangute Beach - Queen of beaches
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus - UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Dudhsagar Falls - Magnificent waterfall
  • Anjuna Flea Market - Shopping paradise
  • Fort Aguada - 17th-century Portuguese fort

Travel Information

Distance from Mumbai: 600 km
Journey Time by Bus: 12 hours
Best For: Beach lovers, party enthusiasts, couples

Rishikesh - Yoga Capital of the World

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Rishikesh is a spiritual center on the banks of the Ganges River.

About Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a city in India's northern state of Uttarakhand, in the Himalayan foothills beside the Ganges River. The river is considered holy, and the city is renowned as a center for studying yoga and meditation. Temples and ashrams (centers for spiritual studies) line the eastern bank around Swarg Ashram, a traffic-free, alcohol-free and vegetarian enclave upstream from Rishikesh town.

Best Time to Visit

September to November and February to May are the best times to visit Rishikesh. The weather is pleasant for outdoor activities and spiritual practices.

Key Attractions

  • Lakshman Jhula - Iconic suspension bridge
  • Triveni Ghat - Sacred bathing spot
  • Beatles Ashram - Where Beatles meditated
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple - Sacred temple
  • White water rafting - Adventure activity

Travel Information

Distance from Delhi: 230 km
Journey Time by Bus: 6-7 hours
Best For: Spiritual seekers, adventure enthusiasts, yoga practitioners
